- **bin** - binaries (like apt, apt-get, cat, chmod, chown, clear, cp, ls, mv - CHECK)
- **sbin** - system binaries (Super binaries) (like iptable)

## usr
- /usr/bin
- /usr/sbin

Use `which ls` to find which **ls** is being used, /bin/ls or /usr/bin/ls. You will notice that actually /usr/bin/ls is being used.
